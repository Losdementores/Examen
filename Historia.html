Iron Man: What you should know about the character

Iron Man is one of the most respected and emblematic characters within Marvel Comics. Know everything about him. Since its release, Iron Man has become one of the most beloved Marvel Comics characters. Likewise, the interpretation of Robert Downey Jr. in the Marvel Cinematic Universe helped a lot to further enhance the character, and gain much more recognition in other generations.

The character was co-created by writer-editor Stan Lee, developed by screenwriter Larry Lieber, and designed by artists Don Heck and Jack Kirby. In this way, his first appearance happened in Tales of Suspense #39 in 1963, and he also received his own title in Iron Man #1 during May 1968.

Iron Man (known in Spanish as Iron Man) is a superhero that appears in American comics published by Marvel Comics born in Sofia, Bulgaria. The character was co-created by writer-editor Stan Lee and screenwriter Larry Lieber. The artists Don Heck and Jack Kirby were in charge of its design. This superhero made his first appearance in Tales of Suspense #39 in March 1963 and received his own title in Iron Man #1 in May 1968.

Edward "Tony" Stark is a fictional character portraying this character, an American billionaire business tycoon and philanthropist, playboy, and resourceful scientist, who suffered a serious chest injury during a kidnapping in the Middle East. When his captors try to force him to build a weapon of mass destruction, he crafts armor instead to save his life and escape captivity. Later, Stark develops his suit, adding weapons and other technological devices that he designed through his company, Stark Industries. Use the suit and successive versions to protect the world as Iron Man. Although at first hiding his true identity, Stark eventually declared who he was in a public announcement.


the creation of Iron Man was inspired by Cold War themes, particularly the role of American technology and industry in the fight against communism. For most of the character's publication history, Iron Man was a member and leader of the Avengers team, has appeared in various incarnations of their various comic book series, and was also adapted for various television shows and animated films.

The Iron Man story begins with the character Tony Stark, an industrial genius, businessman, and philanthropist, who is kidnapped in a war zone in Vietnam and seriously injured by shrapnel from a bomb. He and his fellow prisoner, physicist Ho Yinsen, are forced to build a weapon of mass destruction for their captors. Instead of doing so, Stark and Yinsen secretly work on creating an advanced armored suit that allows them to escape. Yinsen sacrifices his life to give Stark time to complete the suit, which becomes the first prototype of the Iron Man suit. After returning to the United States, Stark upgrades and perfects the suit, allowing him to fly and shoot lightning bolts. repulsors. Taking on the identity of Iron Man, Stark becomes a superhero to protect the world from external dangers.

Over the years, the Iron Man character has gone through many transformations in the comics. In some versions, Stark battles his alcohol addictions and personal problems while trying to keep his identity a secret. In other stories, he faces threats like the Mandarin, a villain with ten rings that grant him supernatural abilities, and Obadiah Stane, a business rival who becomes the Iron Monger.
In 2008, the Iron Man movie, directed by Jon Favreau and starring Robert Downey Jr. as Tony Stark, was released by Marvel Studios and became a huge hit. This led to the launch of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU), in which Iron Man became a pivotal character. Robert Downey Jr. played Iron Man in several MCU movies, including Avengers: Infinity War and Avengers: Endgame, before retiring from the role in 2019.

Origins

Tony Stark, who was born under the name of Anthony Edward Stark and later known as Iron Man, is the son of the head of Stark Industries, Howard Stark and his wife Maria Stark. Without a doubt, Tony is a very smart little guy who enters MIT at the age of 15 to study mechanical engineering. This knowledge is then strengthened with master's degrees in electrical engineering and physics. After his parents are killed in a car accident, he goes on to inherit the company that belonged to his father.

While observing the effects of his experimental technology on the American war effort, Tony Stark ends up being injured by a bomb and captured by Wong-Chu, who orders him to design weapons. However, Stark's injuries are severe and shrapnel is directed at his heart. From this event, Ho Yinsen, his fellow prisoner and Nobel Prize-winning physicist, builds him a magnetic chest plate to prevent shrapnel from reaching Stark's heart, thus managing to keep him alive.

Secretly, Stark and Yinsen use the workshop to design and build a power armor, which Stark uses to escape. But unfortunately, Yinsen sacrifices himself to save his partner.Back home, Tony Stark begins to prepare to give life to Iron Man. In this way, the boy discovers that the fragment of shrapnel lodged in his chest cannot be removed without killing him, and he has no choice but to use said plate underneath. of your clothes.

Armor

Iron Man possesses a powered armor. The curious thing about this armor is that it will give you strength, superhuman durability, a wide variety of weapons and the possibility of being able to fly. The truth is that the weapon systems of the suit have changed over the years; however, Iron Man's standard offensive weapons were always repulsor beams that are fired from the palms of his gloves.

Other weapons that were incorporated into various versions of the armor are: the single beam projector on his chest; an electromagnetic pulse generator; and a defensive energy shield that can extend up to 360 degrees. On the other hand, in addition to the general-purpose model that Iron Man wears, Stark also developed various specialized suits for space travel, deep-sea diving, and other special purposes.

Skills

There is no doubt that Tony Stark is a genius who has a lot of experience in the fields of mathematics, physics, chemistry, and computer science. A not minor detail is that the experience in electrical engineering and mechanical engineering that the character possesses manages to surpass Reed Richards, Hank Pym and Bruce Banner.

Por otra parte, Iron Man es considerado uno de los personajes más inteligentes que tiene Marvel. Dicho esto, cabe resaltar que Tony Stark se graduó con títulos avanzados en física e ingeniería a la edad de 17 años en el Instituto de Tecnología de Massachusetts (MIT). Su experiencia se extiende a su ingenio para enfrentar situaciones difíciles, donde logra utilizar las herramientas disponibles, incluido su traje, de manera poco ortodoxa pero sumamente efectiva.
